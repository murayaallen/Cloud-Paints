Our story

Paint made in Kenya, for Kenya.

Cloudsent Decor Ltd started as an interior design and gypsum fitting company. Customer after customer asked where we sourced our paint — and whether they could buy that same quality for their own projects.

That question became Cloud Paints. Today we formulate, tint and distribute a full range of interior and exterior coatings — every batch lab-tested, every colour tuned for Kenyan light, humidity and architecture.

"Colour consistency and quality are our focus. Our customers can be confident that the colour on their home is exactly what they chose — and paid for."
