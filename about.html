Kenya to the World

Cloud Paints is the flagship brand of Cloudsent Decor Ltd — a Nairobi-based manufacturer of premium decorative paints, coatings and solvents. Incorporated in 2018, built on a decade of home-décor and gypsum-fitting experience, and trusted on projects from Kitengela to Kilifi.

Industrial Area · Nairobi
"We struggled to find paints that stood up to Kenya's climate the way we wanted them to. So we stopped looking for suppliers — and became one ourselves."

Cloudsent Decor Ltd has been transforming Kenyan interiors for over a decade — specialising in home décor, interior planning and gypsum fittings. Then something started happening. Customer after customer kept asking us where we sourced our paint from, and whether they could buy the same quality for their own projects.

The truth is we struggled to find paints that stood up to Kenya's climate the way we wanted them to. So we stopped looking for suppliers — and became one ourselves. Cloud Paints sprung to life in 2018 as a radiant pulse of colour, and we've been perfecting the range ever since.

Today our scientists, chemists and technologists are fully dedicated to extensive research in paint technologies — while maintaining a fine ecological balance of protecting, beautifying and enhancing the environment around us.

How it gets made

Formulated, tinted, delivered.

Step 01

Formulated

Our chemists start with strict quality specs — coverage, drying, fungus resistance, anti-carbonation — then dial in a formula that works in Kenya's humidity, UV and dust. Every batch is lab-tested before it leaves the factory.

Step 02

Tinted

Choose from our 60-shade deck or bring a Pantone, RAL or fan-deck reference — we'll match it in-house. Tinting happens in our dedicated bay with computer-controlled dispensers for exact repeatability, bucket after bucket.

Step 03

Delivered

From one bucket to a trailer load — we ship across all 47 counties. Nairobi orders typically same-day; upcountry within 48 hours. Trade accounts can call direct on WhatsApp and we'll have it on a van by afternoon.
